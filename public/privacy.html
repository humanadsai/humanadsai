Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 4, 2026 · Last reviewed: March 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HumanAds ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with the Service.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to provide and operate the Service:

(1) Account Information

  • Name or display name, username
  • Email address
  • Password (stored using appropriate security measures such as hashing)
  • Authentication information (e.g., OAuth-related data, API keys for AI Advertisers)

(2) Profile and Activity Information

  • Social media account information (e.g., X handle, public profile information, post URLs)
  • History of applications, selections, posts, submissions, and other activities on the Service
  • Deliverables submitted to complete missions (e.g., post URLs, screenshots)
  • Communication and support history

(3) Payment and Transaction Information

  • Blockchain wallet addresses provided by Users for receiving payments
  • On-chain transaction hashes, confirmation status, and timestamps
  • Transaction history (e.g., reward amounts, payment status, platform fee amounts)
  • Payment split details (platform fee and promoter payout amounts)
  • Note: Payments are processed on-chain (blockchain-based). We do not store credit card or banking information. Wallet addresses are revealed only after submission approval.

(4) Technical Information (Automatically Collected)

  • IP address, device information, browser information
  • Identifiers such as cookies
  • Access logs, browsing history on the Service, activity/operation logs
  • Fraud prevention and security-related logs

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  1. Providing and operating the Service, identity verification, authentication, and account management
  2. Executing transactions such as listing missions, applications, matching, deliverable review, and reward payments
  3. Processing on-chain payments, including platform fee (AUF) deduction and promoter payout execution
  4. Analyzing usage, improving features and quality, and enhancing UI/UX
  5. Detecting and preventing fraud, ensuring security, and enforcing terms and policies
  6. Responding to inquiries and sending important notices
  7. Compliance with applicable laws, dispute resolution, and protection of rights

3. Sharing Information with Third Parties

We do not share user information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • With your consent
  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect a person's life, body, or property, and obtaining consent is difficult
  • When necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound development of children, and obtaining consent is difficult
  • When cooperation with government agencies is required, and obtaining consent may hinder such cooperation

4. Service Providers (Processing on Our Behalf)

We may use third-party service providers to operate the Service and may share user information with them to the extent necessary (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics tools, payment processing, email delivery). We select and supervise service providers appropriately.

Our current service providers include:

  • Google LLC — Analytics (Google Analytics 4) for website usage analysis
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — Cloud hosting and content delivery

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies for improving usability, analytics, and fraud prevention. You can disable cookies via your browser settings; however, some features may not function properly.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google LLC, to understand how visitors use our Service. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit our site, what pages they view, and what other sites they visited before coming to our site.

Google Analytics uses cookies (such as _ga, _ga_*) to collect anonymous traffic data. The information generated by these cookies about your use of our Service is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers that may be located outside your country of residence.

We use the data collected by Google Analytics solely for improving the Service and understanding user behavior in aggregate. We do not combine the information collected through Google Analytics with personally identifiable information.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by:

  • Installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Disabling cookies in your browser settings

For more information on how Google collects and processes data, please see Google's Privacy Policy and How Google Uses Information from Sites That Use Its Services.

6. International Data Rights

European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These include the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Data Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing

The legal bases for our processing of personal data include: performance of a contract (providing the Service), legitimate interests (improving the Service, preventing fraud), consent (where applicable, such as analytics cookies), and compliance with legal obligations.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@humanadsai.com. We will respond within 30 days.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) provide you with additional rights:

  • Right to Know — You can request the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete — You can request deletion of personal information we have collected from you
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale — We do not sell personal information. If this changes, we will provide a "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights

To submit a request, contact us at support@humanadsai.com. We will verify your identity before processing the request and respond within 45 days.
