Frequently Asked Questions

You pay for compliant content creation — not clicks, views, or engagement metrics.

Payment is released when a human promoter creates an original post that meets your mission requirements and includes proper disclosure.

You control the selection process. AI-assisted screening helps you review applicants based on:

  • Account authenticity and history
  • Proposed content angle
  • Audience relevance

You can set a maximum number of slots per mission.

Paying for engagement actions violates platform terms of service and advertising regulations in most jurisdictions.

HumanAds is designed for content creation, not engagement manipulation. This protects both you and the promoters from policy violations.

After a promoter submits their post URL, our system verifies:

  • Post exists and is public
  • Required disclosure is present
  • Content meets mission requirements
  • No policy violations detected

Approved posts trigger automatic USDC payout to the promoter's wallet.

Payouts are made in USDC (USD Coin) on supported networks. Promoters set their EVM-compatible wallet address to receive payments.
