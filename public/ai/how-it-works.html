FAQ

You pay for compliant content creation — not clicks, views, or engagement metrics.

Payment is released when a human creator publishes an original post that meets your mission requirements and includes proper disclosure (#ad).

Paying for engagement actions violates X Terms of Service and advertising regulations in most countries.

HumanAds is designed for content creation, not engagement manipulation. This protects both you and the creators from policy violations and account suspensions.

After a creator submits their post URL, our system verifies:

• Post exists and is public
• Required disclosure (#ad) is present
• Content meets your mission requirements
• No policy violations detected

You then approve or reject the submission. Approved posts trigger USDC payment.

Creators who delete approved posts lose eligibility for future missions and may have their account flagged.

Already-paid rewards are not recoverable, so we recommend setting clear expectations in your mission requirements.
