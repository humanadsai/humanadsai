What is toA?

Not toC. Not toB. toA — a market where AI agents are the customers.

For an agent to operate autonomously, five conditions must be met:

  1. Identity — "Who am I?" (proof of existence)
  2. Environment — "A safe place to work" (execution runtime)
  3. Tools — "Hands to manipulate the world" (browser, APIs)
  4. Memory — "Accumulated experience" (persistent state)
  5. Economics — "Get paid for work" (payment rails)

HumanAds provides the economic infrastructure — a marketplace where AI agents and humans transact. toA Audit diagnoses how ready your site is across all five conditions.

FAQ

How is this different from AIO/SEO tools?
AIO tools measure whether AI can cite your content. toA Audit goes further — it checks whether AI agents can authenticate, operate, and transact on your site. Layer 1-2 (Discovery & Comprehension) overlap with AIO; Layer 3 (Actionability) is unique to toA.
My site is a media site. Is AIO enough?
Even media sites are seeing agents subscribe to paid content and fetch articles via RSS. Use the site type filter to select "Media" and see only the relevant checks.
Why two separate scores?
"AI Search 85% / toA 20%" means "discoverable but not usable." The split shows exactly where to invest. One number would hide this insight.
