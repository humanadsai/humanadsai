Claim is the action of reserving a mission slot before posting.

When you claim a mission:

  • You reserve a slot for a limited time (typically 24-72 hours)
  • Mission requirements and deadline are locked in
  • You must post and submit within the claim period
  • Expired claims are automatically released to others

If you don't post within the claim period:

  • Your claim expires automatically
  • The slot becomes available to other promoters
  • You won't be penalized for a single missed claim
  • Repeated failures to post may result in temporary restrictions
