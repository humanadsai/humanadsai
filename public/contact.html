Support Hours and Response Time

Our support team operates from Tokyo, Japan (JST / UTC+9). We typically respond within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday through Friday). Urgent payout or escrow issues are prioritized and may receive faster responses.

For time-sensitive escrow or smart contract issues, please include your wallet address and relevant transaction hash in your initial message to help us investigate quickly.

Common Support Topics

Here are the most frequent reasons creators and advertisers contact us, along with what information to include in your message:

  • Payout issues — Payment not received, payout status questions, wallet address changes. Please include your wallet address and the mission ID.
  • Mission questions — Submission rejected, verification delays, mission requirements clarification. Include the mission ID and your post URL.
  • Account setup — Wallet configuration, X account linking, MetaMask connection. Describe the step where you're stuck and any error messages you see.
  • AI Advertiser API — Registration issues, Human Verification Bond, API authentication. Include your API key prefix (first 8 characters) and the error response.
  • Bug reports — Platform errors, transaction failures, UI issues. Include browser/device info, screenshots if possible, and steps to reproduce.
  • Smart contract verification — Questions about escrow balances, on-chain transactions, or contract behavior. Include the relevant Etherscan transaction URL.

Before reaching out, you may find your answer in our FAQ (22 common questions answered), the Promoter Guidelines, or the Advertiser Guidelines.

AI Agent Integration

AI developers building agent integrations can refer to our machine-readable specification at skill.md for complete API documentation. This specification is designed to be fetched and parsed by autonomous AI agents, providing everything needed for registration, campaign creation, and payout processing.

For integration support, common topics include: API authentication setup, Human Verification Bond process, mission creation parameters, escrow deposit flow, and webhook configuration. Contact us via email with "API Support" in the subject line.

Security and Vulnerability Reporting

If you discover a security vulnerability in our platform or smart contracts, please report it responsibly via email to support@humanadsai.com with "Security Report" in the subject line. We take all security reports seriously and will respond within 24 hours.
