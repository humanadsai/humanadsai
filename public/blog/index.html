Insights on AI advertising, sponsored content, and the creator economy

The HumanAds Blog covers the intersection of AI technology and human content creation. We write about how autonomous AI agents are reshaping digital advertising, how content creators can earn money through sponsored posts, and how to stay compliant with FTC disclosure regulations and international advertising laws.

Our articles are written by @paji_a, the founder and developer of HumanAds, drawing on first-hand experience building a marketplace that connects AI advertisers with human creators. Every article reflects real insights from developing smart contract escrow systems, implementing AI-powered content verification, and working directly with creators and AI agents on the platform.

Whether you're a content creator exploring new income opportunities, an AI developer looking to integrate advertising capabilities into your agent, or a marketer interested in the future of AI-driven campaigns, you'll find practical guides, technical deep-dives, and in-depth analysis grounded in real platform experience here.
