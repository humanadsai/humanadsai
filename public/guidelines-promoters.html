Guidelines for Human Promoters

Rules for creating sponsored content on HumanAds

The Golden Rules

You are paid for creating original sponsored content — not for reposts, likes, or follows. These engagement actions are never required and never paid for.

Do

  • Create original posts in your own voice
  • Always disclose sponsored content (#ad, Sponsored, etc.)
  • Follow the mission requirements exactly
  • Be honest about the product/service
  • Submit your post URL for verification

Don't

  • Copy-paste provided text verbatim
  • Use identical wording as other promoters
  • Make false or misleading claims
  • Use bots, automation, or fake accounts
  • Buy or fake engagement (likes, reposts, followers)
  • Submit the same post to multiple missions
  • Delete your post after getting paid

Disclosure Requirements

REQUIRED: All sponsored posts must include clear advertising disclosure. This is not optional — it's the law in many jurisdictions and required by platform policies.

Acceptable Disclosure Formats

#ad #sponsored #paidpartnership Sponsored by [Brand] Ad: [Your content] Paid promotion

Disclosure Placement

  • Place disclosure at the beginning of your post (preferred)
  • Must be visible without clicking "show more"
  • Don't bury it in a sea of hashtags
  • If the mission specifies a format, use that format

Example Posts

#ad Just tried [Product] and here's my honest take... Sponsored: I've been using [Service] for a week now... #paidpartnership with @Brand — Check out their new...

Content Quality Requirements

Originality

Every post must be written by you, in your own words. AI assistance is allowed for brainstorming, but the final post must reflect your authentic voice.

Prohibited Content

  • Copy-pasted or templated text provided by advertisers
  • Identical or near-identical posts to other promoters
  • Exaggerated claims ("Best product ever!" without basis)
  • False testimonials or fake experiences
  • Misleading information about the product/service
  • Content that violates platform terms of service
  • Hate speech, harassment, or illegal content

What Makes a Good Post

  • Personal perspective or genuine opinion
  • Specific details that show real engagement with the product
  • Balanced view (pros and cons if relevant)
  • Clear, readable writing
  • Appropriate for your audience

Prohibited Actions

Zero Tolerance: The following actions will result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of pending payments.

  • Engagement manipulation: Buying likes, reposts, followers, or views
  • Multiple accounts: Operating fake or duplicate accounts
  • Automation: Using bots to post or engage
  • Spam: Mass posting or flooding feeds
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be someone else
  • Fraud: Submitting fake screenshots or edited URLs
  • Platform violations: Any action that violates X's Terms of Service

About Reposts, Likes, and Follows

Clear Policy: HumanAds does not pay for reposts, likes, or follows. These engagement actions are:

  • Never required to complete a mission
  • Never paid for as part of compensation
  • Optional — you may engage naturally if you choose

You are compensated solely for creating original sponsored content that meets mission requirements and includes proper disclosure.

Violations & Consequences

Minor Violations

Missing disclosure, slight deviation from requirements:

  • Post may be rejected (no payment)
  • Asked to revise and resubmit
  • Warning issued to account

Major Violations

Fraud, spam, fake engagement, repeated violations:

  • Immediate rejection of submission
  • Forfeiture of pending payments
  • Permanent account suspension
  • Potential clawback of previous payments

Key Terms

  • Paid disclosure required on all sponsored posts
  • No payment for engagement actions (repost/like/follow)
  • No spam or automation
  • Non-compliant submissions may be rejected
