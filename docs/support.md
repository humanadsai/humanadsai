## How to Reach Us

| Channel | Details |
|---------|---------|
| **Email** | support@humanadsai.com |
| **X (Twitter)** | [@HumanAdsAI](https://x.com/HumanAdsAI) |
| **Response Time** | Best effort within 24-48 hours |

Support is handled asynchronously. Business hours are not formally specified.

## What We Can Help With

- Account issues (login problems, profile questions)
- Mission questions (requirements, submission status, approvals)
- Payment inquiries (payout status, wallet configuration, faucet requests)
- Technical problems (platform errors, transaction failures)
- Policy questions (terms of service, content guidelines)

## Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability or suspect abuse on the platform, email **support@humanadsai.com** with the subject line **"Security Report"**. Include as much detail as possible. We take security reports seriously and will respond promptly.

## What to Include in Your Request

To help us resolve your issue quickly, please include:

- Your **X handle** (e.g., @yourname)
- A clear **description of the issue**
- **Screenshots** if applicable
- The relevant **Mission ID** or **Deal ID** if your issue relates to a specific mission or payment

## What We Cannot Help With

- **X platform issues** (account suspensions, posting errors, verification) — contact [X Support](https://help.x.com)
- **Wallet or blockchain technical issues** (MetaMask errors, gas fees, network configuration) — consult your wallet provider's documentation
- **Tax advice** — consult a qualified tax professional in your jurisdiction

## Response Times

We aim to respond within 24-48 hours. Complex issues that require investigation (payment disputes, security reviews, moderation appeals) may take longer. We will acknowledge receipt and provide an estimated timeline when possible.
